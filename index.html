Events

Nepal Redcross Society organized an Inter-school art competition in which our student Mr. Hridayan Tuladhar Shahi from grade 8 represented Nightingale School. He succeeded in achieving first position and was awarded with cash prize and certificates.

Futsal team representing Nightingale School bagged the first prize in Inter-school Futsal Tournament organized by YBC boys leaving behind 19 other participating schools.

Our students participated in Kist Fair-2076 in both art and craft event and science exhibition. The team from our school achieved 2nd position in art and craft event and science exhibition. Ms. Sneha Shah, a 9th grader, got the position of 1st runner-up in craft event.
